# ⏱️ Email Rate Limiting

## How It Works

### Rate Limit: 20 emails per hour

- **Upload Excel:** First 20 students get emails immediately
- **Remaining students:** Marked as "Pending"
- **Delay:** 3 minutes between each email
- **Safe for Gmail:** Avoids spam filters and account blocks

## Example Timeline

### If you upload 100 students:

**First Hour (0-60 min):**
- 20 emails sent
- 80 pending

**Second Hour (60-120 min):**
- Click "Send All Emails" again
- Next 20 emails sent
- 60 pending

**Continue this pattern...**

## Why Rate Limiting?

✅ **Prevents Gmail blocks:** Too many emails = account suspension
✅ **Avoids spam filters:** Gradual sending looks more natural
✅ **Professional approach:** Industry standard practice
✅ **Safe & reliable:** Your account stays secure

## Manual Control

You can:
1. Upload Excel (first 20 sent automatically)
2. Wait 1 hour
3. Click "Send All Emails" (next 20 sent)
4. Repeat until all sent

## UI Features

- **Rate limit banner:** Shows 20 emails/hour limit
- **Pending count:** See how many emails waiting
- **Status badges:** Track sent vs pending
- **Beautiful icons:** Circular action buttons

## Tips

- Upload in morning, emails send throughout day
- Check "Pending" count in statistics
- Use "Send All" button every hour for next batch
- All automatic with 3-minute delays

Perfect for managing large student lists safely! 🎯
